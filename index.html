Nel Abdiel

I was born and raised in Puerto Rico, where I went to school at the University of Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras Campus, completing a B.S. in Mathematics. After completing my undergraduate studies, I went to the University of Northern Iowa and obtained my M.A. in Pure Mathematics. I began my Ph.D. in Pure Mathematics at Florida State University and after my first year I transferred to the University of Iowa. I will be receiving my Ph.D. in May 2016. This past Winter I was accepted as a fellow at the Data Incubator where I became certified in Data Science.
